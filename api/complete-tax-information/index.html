Complete Tax Information

Add W8/W9 tax information to an existing investor passport. See more information here Tax Form Guidelines:

  • Use W-9 template if investor is an Individual and the investor lives in the United States
  • Use W-9-E template if investor is an Entity and the investor lives in the United States
  • Use W-BEN template if investor is an Individual and the investor does not live in the United States
  • Use W-BEN-E template if investor is an Entity and the investor does not live in the United States
